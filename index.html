What is Screen Diet

According to research company Counterpoint, half of users spend more than five hours on smartphones, and 26% spend more than seven hours a day.

Whitney Jones, a licensed clinical social worker, advises implementing the screen diet. You can experiment and try to spend at least an hour a day without a phone, without checking mail and notifications, or turn off your phone one day a week on your day off, watching how it affects your state of mind.

3 rules of a healthy body

Sleeping mode

Analyze the quality of sleep, make the necessary changes if required. Those who want to rebuild the regime in the least painless way for their body are advised to give up watching TV and using gadgets before going to bed

Drink water

Before meals, for 15-20 minutes, be sure to drink 1-2 glasses of water. If you are preparing dinner at home or ordering it from a cafe, ask to bring it before serving. This will help you eat less and improve your metabolism.
